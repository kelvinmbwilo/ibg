Ministries

Men's Ministry

Knowing that men also has got special things to share through teachings and prayer sessions so as to complete the body of Christ, LHCC has established a special ministry for men so as to make them take their responsibility as men at the church as well as fathers at home.

Charity Ministry

Understanding the need of people around us, LHCC has established a charity ministry which is dedicated to help people in need to fulfill their needs as well as sharing with them the wonderful of the love of God through showing them that for his strikes we were healed and that was the end of every suffering.

Student Ministry

This ministry deals with taking care of students so that they can be in a good condition to perform their academic activities as well as preparing them for the bright future as well as taking their responsibilities in various areas so as to build the Kingdom of God as well as the Nation.


Women's Ministry

This ministry deals with women's affairs for the kingdom of God. This is all about the teachings as well as experience sharing for women at LHCC through fellowship and prayer meetings.

Children's Ministry

God has given us children as gift, so it is our responsibilty to keep them and raise them in his own accord as directed in the holy Bible. We have got good and qualified team for children ministry.

Family Ministry

We believe in family as a unity for the church fellowship. Prio to the importance of family we have the special ministry for family so as to maintain the church fellowship from the family level for the Glory of God.

Youth Ministry

This ministry deals with youth affairs where by it acts as a platform for youth to share their views as well as having fellowship for Godly teachings and prayer meetings
