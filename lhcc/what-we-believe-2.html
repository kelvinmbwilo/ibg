What We Believe

LHCC, we believe the following

  • Jesus is the only way to heaven
  • Jesus is God
  • Jesus died and rose again and he will come to take us
  • Every one has God a godly gift and talent to excel in the world
  • Every one has got a Godly destiny during his life time in the world
  • We believe in holy trinity
  • We believe in holiness as the only ticket to heaven
  • We believe in fellowship as the way to serve God
